Frequently Ask Questions

What is the SideWalk Safty study?

The SideWalk Safty study aims to find out how we can better detect and monitor bad sidewalk condition.

Who can participate in this study

Anyone! You can participate if you find out sidewalk issues that may hamper the normal walk.

Who can see my information?

Only the study team will have access to the information you provided. Your privacy is extremely important to us!

Will I be contacted after the study?

If you provide your email address, we will send you updates about your participation in the study.
